What we do

Spectrum started in a tiny little shop in Northern Colorado nearly 17 years ago. Mark was convinced that you could blend the durability of powder coating with the decorative aspects of painting. No one was doing it back then (or now for that matter), and after a few tries, Mark understood why. When we started, artistic and powder coating were in the same category as oil and water. Thank goodness he kept at it, because what resulted was the nucleus of the spectrum process. Multi-color schemes, panels, fades, custom logos – all done in powder. If powder coating isn’t what suits you, no problem – we do wet painting as well. From full on liquid paint jobs, or just matching your carbon gadgets to your powder coated frame, we can cover it. To see what we can do, check out the powder or paint section for current projects, or check out one of the galleries for past projects, including some of our motorcycle projects, signs, and sculptures.
